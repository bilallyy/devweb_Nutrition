Food Groups

There are five main food groups that are important for maintaining a healthy diet:

  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Grains
  • Protein
  • Dairy

It is recommended that adults consume a certain number of servings from each food group each day. Here is a general guideline:

Food Group Servings per day
Fruits 2 cups
Vegetables 2.5 cups
Grains 6 ounces
Protein 5.5 ounces
Dairy 3 cups

Healthy Recipes

Grilled Chicken and Vegetable Kabobs

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Directions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Thread chicken, zucchini, bell pepper, and onion onto skewers.
  3. Brush skewers with olive oil and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until chicken is cooked through.

Serve with rice or quinoa for a healthy, balanced meal.

Portion Control

Portion control is an important part of maintaining a healthy diet. Here are some tips:

  • Use smaller plates and bowls to control your portion sizes.
  • Measure your food to ensure you are eating the correct portion size.
  • Don't eat straight from the bag or container. Portion out your food onto a plate or bowl.
  • Eat slowly and savor your food. This will help you feel full and satisfied.

Eating Out

Eating out can be a challenge when you are trying to eat healthy, but it is possible. Here are some tips:

  • Look for healthy options on the menu, such as grilled or roasted meats, vegetables, and salads.
  • Ask for dressings and sauces on the side so you can control how much you eat.
  • Avoid fried foods and dishes with a lot of cheese or cream.
  • Share your meal with a friend or take half of it home for later.

Benefits

Eating a healthy diet has many benefits:

  • Helps maintain a healthy weight
  • Reduces the risk of chronic diseases, such as heart disease and diabetes
  • Provides essential nutrients for overall health
  • Improves mood and energy levels
  • Helps you feel and look your best

Healthy Snacks

Snacking can be part of a healthy diet, as long as you choose the right snacks. Here are some healthy options:

  • Fruits and vegetables with hummus or nut butter
  • Plain Greek yogurt with berries and nuts
  • Air-popped popcorn
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Homemade trail mix with nuts, seeds, and dried fruit
